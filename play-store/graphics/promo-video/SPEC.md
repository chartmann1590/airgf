# Promo Video (optional)

Play Store promo videos are hosted on YouTube — you don't upload a video file
to Play Console, just paste the URL.

- **File**: `youtube-url.txt` — put the final YouTube link in this file once
  the video is uploaded (unlisted or public).
- Recommended length: 30 seconds - 2 minutes.
- Keep content PG per the same rules as screenshots/feature graphic — this is
  the most heavily scrutinized asset for policy review on companion apps.
