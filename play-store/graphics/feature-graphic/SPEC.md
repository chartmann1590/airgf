# Feature Graphic

- **File**: `feature-graphic-1024x500.png` (or .jpg)
- **Size**: 1024x500 px exactly
- **Format**: PNG or JPEG, no alpha
- Shown at the top of the store listing on some layouts, and in featured
  placements — should work as a banner (avoid critical text near edges,
  keep safe area centered).

Keep the tone PG: brand mark + tagline (e.g. "Amoura — Your AI Companion"),
no explicit/suggestive imagery. Play's automated scanners flag suggestive
feature graphics even if the app itself is compliant.
