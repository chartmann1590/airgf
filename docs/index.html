AirGF

Your AI companion, entirely on your device.

Private. Personal. Powered by AI.

AirGF is an on-device AI companion app for Android. No cloud, no subscriptions, no data leaving your phone. Powered by Gemma 4 with 3D avatars, voice, and image sharing.

Download APK View Source
🧠

On-Device AI

Gemma 4 E2B runs entirely on your phone. No internet needed for conversations. Your chats never leave your device.

👤

3D Avatars

14 unique 3D character models with real-time emotion-driven expressions, lip sync, and idle animations.

🖼

Image Sharing

Send photos from your gallery or camera. Your AI companion understands images and can generate pictures with on-device Stable Diffusion.

🔊

Voice & TTS

Multiple voice profiles with real-time text-to-speech and word-level lip sync animation on the 3D avatar.

🎭

Personality

Choose from 8 personality traits, set relationship type, and customize communication style. She adapts to you.

🔒

100% Private

Everything runs on-device. No accounts, no cloud APIs, no telemetry. Your companion is yours alone.
