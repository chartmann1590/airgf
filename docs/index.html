Features

Everything runs on your phone.

🧠

Gemma 4 On-Device

A 2-billion parameter AI model runs directly on your phone. No internet needed. Conversations are instant and private.

👤

3D Avatars

14 unique characters with real-time expressions, emotion-driven animation, and lip-synced speech.

📸

Image Sharing

Send photos and she understands them. She can generate images too, powered by on-device Stable Diffusion.

🎙

Voice & Lip Sync

4 distinct voice profiles with word-level timing that drives lip sync animation on the 3D avatar in real time.

✨

Deep Personality

8 personality traits, multiple relationship types, and communication styles. She remembers and adapts.

🔔

Proactive Messages

She reaches out on her own with messages throughout the day. Configurable frequency from rarely to often.

Privacy is not a feature. It's the architecture.

There is no server. There is no account. There is no analytics SDK. The AI model, your conversations, and your data exist only on your device.

No cloud No accounts No telemetry No subscriptions
