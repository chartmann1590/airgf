Features

Everything runs on your phone.

🧠

Gemma 4 On-Device

A 2-billion parameter AI model runs directly on your phone. No internet needed. Conversations are instant and private.

👤

3D Avatars

14 unique characters with real-time expressions, emotion-driven animation, and lip-synced speech.

📸

Image Sharing

Send photos and your companion understands them, and can generate images too, powered by on-device Stable Diffusion.

🎙

Voice & Lip Sync

4 distinct voice profiles with word-level timing that drives lip sync animation on the 3D avatar in real time.

✨

Deep Personality

Choose a girlfriend, boyfriend, or partner. 8 personality traits, multiple relationship types, and communication styles — they remember and adapt to you.

🔔

Proactive Messages

Your companion reaches out on their own with messages throughout the day. Configurable frequency from rarely to often.

Your conversations stay on your device.

There is no server and no account required. The AI model, your conversations, and your companion's memory exist only on your device. The app shows ads and offers an optional subscription to support development — see our privacy policy for exactly what that involves.
